Our Vision

Cities that listen,
systems that act

Project CivicLens bridges the gap between citizens and civic administration — turning scattered reports into an intelligent, visual resolution pipeline that every stakeholder can trust.

🗺️

Visual Issue Mapping

Every reported issue is geo-pinned on a live map, categorised by type, and made visible to both citizens and officials in real time.

⚡

Intelligent Routing

Issues are automatically assigned to the correct department using smart rules, zone mapping, and priority scoring.

🔍

Full Transparency

Citizens can track the status of every issue from report to resolution, rebuilding trust in local governance.
